Accordion (FAQ)

We offer a full refund for cancellations made up to 30 days before the tour date. A 50% refund is available for cancellations made between 15 and 29 days prior. No refunds are provided for cancellations within 14 days of the tour.

Our standard packages do not include international airfare. However, we can assist you with booking flights through our travel partners to ensure you get the best rates and convenient schedules.
